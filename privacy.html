This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use Mindric ("the App"), a service provided by Mindric Pharmaceuticals Ltd. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our App. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use the App, we collect the following types of information: Personal Information: We collect personal information you provide when you register for an account. This information includes facility names (hospitals, pharmacies), key person names, email addresses, phone numbers, and banking details for account registration and credit accounts management.

Transaction Information: When you make a purchase, return or request a quotation through the App, we collect information about the transaction, such as product details, purchase price, and the date of the transaction.

Usage and Device Information: We collect information about how you use the App and information about your device such as the device's Internet Protocol address, operating system, and browser type.

Use of App Description

We use App Description and similar technologies to enhance your experience with the App, provide live support, help us understand how the App is being used, and provide a safer experience. We use both first-party and third-party App Description for these purposes.

How we use your information

We use the personal information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve the App. This includes using the information to:

  • Process transactions, send you related information, including confirmations and invoices, and manage inventory.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests and provide customer service. Communicate with you about products, services, offers, and promotions
Information Sharing

We may share your personal information in the following circumstances: With service providers who process data on our behalf, such as payment service providers and providers of live customer support.

When we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Mindric, our users, or the public.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction or accidental loss, alteration, unauthorized disclosure or access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can do this by contacting us at mindricsystems@gmail.com

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
